Animated explainer video: Netwerkpunt Radar

School assignment: For this assignment the students had to work with different clients. We were split into different groups. My group received an assignment for Netwerkpunt Radar and was responsible for creating an explainer video for general practitioners. My group consisted of two animators and one illustrator. I made the character design while my teammates animated the characters for the explainer video. Remark: the video below is in DUTCH.
