Animation video: Me, Myself & Why

School assignment: Create an animation video about something important in your life. I made a video about how I sometimes feel lonely when I'm by myself. This feeling disappears when my sister comes in the room. In this video I illustrated my sister coming in with a bowl of ice cream to cheer me up.
